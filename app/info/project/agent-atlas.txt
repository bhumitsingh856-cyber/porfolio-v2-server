Agent Atlas
Research Done Right. In Minutes.
Research shouldn't take forever. Introducing Agent Atlas—an AI research assistant that actually researches, so you don't have to.

Watch as intelligent agents break down your topic, investigate in parallel, synthesize findings, and generate a comprehensive report with citations , embedded images , mermaid diagrams. Then ask follow-up questions using RAG-powered semantic search.

From hours to minutes. From scattered to structured. From guesswork to insight.

🎯 The Problem
Research today is broken:

❌ Hours spent googling and verifying
❌ ChatGPT hallucinations you can't trust
❌ Copy-pasting from 20 different sources
❌ No way to ask follow-ups
❌ Information scattered everywhere
There has to be a better way.

✨ The Solution
Agent Atlas uses multi-agent AI orchestration to:

Break down your research topic intelligently
Research in parallel with specialized agents
Generate structured reports with citations, images, and diagrams
Chat with your research using advanced RAG
Get answers instantly with streaming responses
All in 2-5 minutes. Automated. Reliable. Verifiable.

🚀 What You Get
📊 Intelligent Reports
Structured, well-organized findings
Embedded citations & sources
Visual diagrams (Mermaid)
Professional formatting

💬 Chat with Your Research
Ask follow-up questions naturally
Get instant answers powered by semantic search

⚡ Real-Time Execution
Watch agents research in real-time
Monitor progress live
Transparent, no black boxes

Your Question
↓
Agents Break It Down (Intelligent Planning)
↓
Multiple Researchers Investigate in Parallel
↓
Multiple Workers Synthesize Findings and write their sections
↓
